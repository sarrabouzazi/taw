TAW 1.0 /Demo day

The 8 founders representing the 4 targeted African countries got invited to Addis Ababa to pitch their ideas for a chance to win a grant prize of 7 000 USD to help them launch their project. The Demo Day was preceded by a startup ecosystem tour in which the finalists visited a coworking space (Blue Space), an innovation hub (Creative Hub) and met with successful Local startups. Based on four criteria; impact, market size, team and competitive advantage, the startups were scored by judges and UJANA was announced as a winner of TAW 1.0.
