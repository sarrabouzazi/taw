TAW 1.0: A 5-month entrepreneurship program targeting female-led & idea-stage tech startups in Ethiopia, Senegal, Tanzania & Tunisia providing solutions to the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs). It offers teams an exclusive opportunity to transform their ideas into reality, acquire new skills and be part of a community of empowered African entrepreneurs.
