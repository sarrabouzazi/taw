TAW 1.0 /Bootcamps

Bootcamp timeline

Starting August 2022, a series of 3-day bootcamps were organized in Tunis, Dakar, Dar Es Salaam, and Addis Ababa
where participating female entrepreneurs were offered training as an introduction to key skills they
need (market analysis, product management, pitching, Business Model Canvas...) and in preparation for
the pitching competition on the last day.

4 bootcamps were organized respectively:



The Dot Tunis 17-18-19 of August



D-Hub, Dakar on 24-25-26 of August



Seedspace, Dar Es Salaam on 29-30-31 of August.



ADORE Addis Hotel, Addis Ababa on 7-8-9 of September.

2 Winners per Bootcamp !

The winning team in each one of the four selected cities won a 2K usd grant.
The two best pitches had access to a 12-weeks online incubation program
and a fully funded trip to Addis Ababa to pitch in the final ceremony.
